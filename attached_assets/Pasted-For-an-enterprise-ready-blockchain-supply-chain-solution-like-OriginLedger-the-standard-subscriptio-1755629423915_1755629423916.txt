For an enterprise-ready blockchain supply chain solution like OriginLedger, the standard subscription pricing model typically includes tiered plans based on users, assets, and feature sets. Here’s a realistic structure for 2025:

Enterprise SaaS Subscription Pricing Benchmarks
Free Trial:

14–30 days with full feature access (limited users/assets).

Goal: Convert enterprise pilot teams by demonstrating ROI.

Common Paid Plans (per user per month or per asset per month)
Plan Type	Typical Price (2025)	Features
Business	$50–100/user/month	Advanced tracking, reporting, integrations, priority email support, up to 500 assets
Enterprise	$150–200+/user/month	Custom integrations, SSO, dedicated account manager, advanced security, multi-region support, unlimited assets
Asset-Based	$0.50–$2/asset/month	Large enterprise, volume pricing for high asset counts
Custom/Private	Negotiated — $5,000–50,000+/mo	API-only, on-premises, or consortia; unlimited users, audit tools, custom SLA
Annual contracts are standard, often with a discount (8–15%) for yearly pre-payments.

Implementation/setup fees ($5,000–$20,000) may apply for enterprise onboarding, but many SaaS providers waive these for long-term or high-value clients.

Feature Triggers for Higher Pricing
On-chain document storage or complex integrations (ERP, IoT)

AI audit or compliance automation (GDPR/ESG features)

Dedicated support, private tenant deployments

Strategic Options
Start Enterprise at ~$150/user/month or $1/asset/month; offer volume discounts for >100 users/assets.

Target ROI outcomes in your sales pitch: fraud reduction, process efficiency, audit-readiness.

Consider usage-based pricing for very large enterprises, or custom contracts for regulatory-heavy industries.

Bundle additional compliance or analytics modules as premium add-ons.

Final Advice
Enterprises expect:

Per-user/month contracts with advanced features, priority support, security

Transparent upgrade path and clear value for additional spend

Easy pilot-to-paid transition (seamless conversion from trial)

Review your competitors and verticals, then refine based on value delivered and willingness-to-pay interviews as you onboard early clients. This ensures a competitive and resonant pricing structure for OriginLedger.
